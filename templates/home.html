About This Project


Beginning on Wednesday, 2nd August, 2023, this project was handed to me by the team over at Physio AI. As a high school student in year 12, I did have experience using python as a language, but none whatsoever with Django and JSON...


Coincidentally, the project deliverables required that I create this website, complete with a user registration and login functionality, using those very technologies. Thus began my 5 day learning spree!


Each day since then, I have progressed bit by bit, learning the technicalities of the Django framework and JSON. The learning curve was definitely not an easy ride - at times, even demotivating and infuriating, but alas, it is the patience and determiantion of a programmer that pulls us through the end.



What you just interacted with and what you see now are the result of the endless hours I spent scouring Django documentations, Stack Overflow threads, and YouTube tutorials.


Thank you for being here!
